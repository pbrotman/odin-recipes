Parker's Salad

Description

Inspired by cucumber and tomato salads made across the Eastern Mediterranean and Middle East, this salad is sure to rock your world.

Ingredients

  • 6 Persian cucumber (small kind you get in 6-packs)
  • 5 Tomato on vine
  • 1 Yellow onion
  • .5-1 Curly parsley bunch
  • 1 Lemon
  • 5 Tbl Olive oil

If you do not plan to eat a lot, it is recommended to make half the specified amount.

Steps

  1. Dice or chop cucumber, tomato, onion, and parsley. Combine in bowl.
  2. Juice lemon. Add lemon juice and olive oil to bowl.
  3. Mix and serve.
