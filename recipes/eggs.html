Over-Medium Eggs

(Pic from internet)

Description

Eggs are an easy and excellent source of protein in the morning. Over-medium is the perfect preparation for those who want a hard white, but runny yolk.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbl oil

Steps

  1. Add oil to pan. Heat on stove to medium high.
  2. As oil has just started to boil or smoke, crack eggs into pan. Turn down heat to medium.
  3. When underside of eggs are cooked, flip and turn heat to low.
  4. Once egg whites are cooked entirely through, turn off stove and serve eggs.
